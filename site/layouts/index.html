About Us

♦

Entrada means "the entrance". It also means "a journey into unexplored territory". We're an early stage accelerator firm that helps companies make the transition from a "scrappy startup" to a "real company" – one that meets the specific criteria and milestones expected by professional venture capitalists.

Years of Expertise

We are veteran angel investors and entrepreneurs who have years of experience and have been on both sides of the fundraising table.

Proven Record

We have raised hundreds of millions of dollars in equity investment and have also invested millions of our own in startups. See our investments to learn more

Risk Takers

If we think an idea has the potential to succeed, we're ready and willing to take that risk in the early stage to help it grow into a Stage A ready company.

Getting your "Series A” round is more than just hitting a particular revenue threshold.

Our Services

✻

We invest in people, not products. We won't just write checks, or look at dozens of startups each quarter, rather we work personally and intensely with no more than 10 companies each year helping them develop from late seed stage companies to their first round of professional financing. Here are a few of the things our team helps with:

Pitch and Pitch Deck Perfection

You often get one shot with an investor. We want to ensure you’re in the best position to capitalize on any opportunity that comes your way.

Talent acquisition

You might have nailed product market fit, but without the right team in place your business can only go so far. Let us leverage our network to help you build the perfect team.

Intellectual Property Protection

Why you continue to focus on building your business, we can help you protect the assets you invested so much time and energy creating.

Cap Table Clean Up

It won’t help you sell more product or find more customers, but it will help streamline the fundraising process by eliminating a friction point with many investors. Our experience in this area will simplify your fundraising efforts.

KPI Determination and Monitoring

Establishing key performance indicators upfront will help you and your team focus on what’s important and make it easier for investors to gain confidence in your business.

Intro to Appropriate VC Firms

When the time is right we can help you navigate the venture world and not just make introductions, but point you to the appropriate firms and partners based on your business and stage.

Meet Our Entrepreneurs

♦

Tammy Camp

An entrepreneur since the age of 21, Tammy has taken part in numerous successful business endeavors, she also manages media buys and serves as an advisor to numerous startups in the security, consumer Internet and executive research fields. After graduating the GSP11 program at Singularity University at NASA Ames and serving as a United States delegate for the 2013 YEA G20 Summit in Moscow, Russia and participant for the US Department of State’s “New Beginning: Entrepreneurship and Business Innovation” program, she combined her experiences to found Stronghold, a distributed exchange which offers the ability to trade cryptocurrency. She holds a world record in kiteboarding, has been featured in Forbes, USA Today, NPR and the International Business Times and was recently included in Forbes 40 under 40.

Paul Scanlan

Paul Scanlan is a passionate digital media entrepreneur, an Emmy Winner, the Founder & President of MobiTV, and is the Founder and CEO of Legion M, the first fan owned entertainment company. Legion M is using disruptive new equity crowdfunding laws to build an entertainment company owned by fans. Fans are the ones who buy the tickets, pay the subscriptions, and fuel the multi-trillion dollar global entertainment industry. Individually, each of us is just a consumer, but together we have the power to shape the future of Hollywood.

Tooey Courtemanche

I've always been a builder at heart. From a young age I was fascinated by construction. I learned that I was drawn to people who value hard work, and who see building as their craft and calling. The need for Procore came from actual problems I was having as the owner on a construction project to build a new home for my family. My construction experience, combined with my knowledge of software development, seemed to be solving a big problem. That's when Procore was born. Procore didn't take off right away. After a few years of building the company, I almost lost my shirt, my home, and just about everything else. In the years since, Procore has grown to be the most used construction software application on the planet. And it's my sincere intention, and that of the hundreds of Procore employees with whom I have the pleasure of working, to continue that growth.


Tooey Courtemanche, Founder and CEO
